Recent Work

Bronx Science Course Helper

The Bronx Science Course Helper was inspired by the annoyance I felt when having to go back in forth between pages of the school course handbook and having to write down courses that I'm interested in. Not only that but we would also have to manually check prerequistes to see if we are allowed to take the course. I built this app to allow students to automatically check prerequistes, save/bookmark classes they are interested in and even allow students to share ratings about the class itself

Ambulance Prediction

The Ambulance prediction website was something I worked on in a group that shows how Machine Learning can help ambulance identify the severity of the injury immedietly but also transport them to the nearest hospital that has the capacity to actually help them. Many times, ambulances might take the patient to the nearest hospital but at times the hospital capacity is full and they are unable to give the attention the patient needs. Using this application, they can predict which hospital has the accomodations for them (and routes them based on how severe the injury).

Roman Numeral Converter

Allows you to give a file of roman numerals and the program will convert the roman numerals to its arabic numeral counterpart and then display the numerals in order (sorted) but also have it in an unsorted column for the users convience.

Retro Text Gaming

A hand held console which shows text based stories and options for the user to chose from to lead to their choice based ending. Our Retro Text Gaming makes use of arduino, an LCD screen to display the story and options and a Joystick give the users the feel of playing an old game.

8 Queens

Student Loan Calculator

An app that lets student calculate their student loans using the interest rate, principle rate and how much they plan on paying on a monthly basis. Shows them how much they are paying past the original loan depending on the rate at which the pay it back.
